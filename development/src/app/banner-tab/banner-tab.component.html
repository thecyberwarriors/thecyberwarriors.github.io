Our Mission

The Cyber Warriors assists Veterans, Active Military, National Guard, and Reservist with cybersecurity careers by identifying the organizations that provide training and certifications, and the institutions that have cybersecurity positions available. We work behind the scenes to build mutually beneficial relationships, introducing candidates to experts with the inside scoop on technology trends and who's hiring.

How We Help

Whether you're leaving the military or just looking for your next position, our experts help you meet your goals on an individual basis. Interested in cybersecurity careers? We connect heroes to hiring managers and IT training resources. Register now!
